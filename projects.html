Research Interests

My work is mostly related to Condensed Matter Physics. My interests especially align with Quantum Dynamics, studying low dimensional systems and Many Body Theory. My current work though is on String Theory. Although it is unrelated to Condensed Matter Physics, my interest in High Energy Physics and Quantum Field Theory allow me to work with a novel perspective on Condensed Matter Physics. I am also interested in work allied with Condensed Matter Physics in the realm of Material Sciences.
